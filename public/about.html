About "Relicious"

What is Relicious?

Concept

Have LOCAL dining with LOCAL people.

"Relicious" is a platform for foreign travelers to find the local people to have local food together, and for local people to find chances to communicate with foreign people through thier daily dinings.

Vision

We want to connect people around the world, and make our world better and smaller.

Food, is one of the biggest expectations for travelers, and one of daily lives for local people.
On the other hand, it's very difficult for foreign travelers to find nice local places to eat, especially which are popular among local people, also, it's very difficult for local people to get a chance to communicate with foreign people, even though they know that it gives them wider vision and chances to connect to other world.

We built this platform to solve these problems and make the world better.
It will be very easy for foreign travelers to get local information, and for local people to get a chance to communicate with foreign people, if they are in the same place(website) sharing their aims each other.

We hope more people can have a key to the world with Relicious.

How does it work?

At first… it's all for FREE!

For foreign travelers

1. Find local people and ask them to have dining with

You can find local people with "Find people" feature of Relicious. Just select city or country name and search, then there will be a list of local people. You can see their picture, age, languages they can speak, interests, and so on. You can have dining with local people whom you want.

2. Join meetups created by other users

You can find meetups which you can join with "Find meetups" feature of Relicious If you don't feel like finding people and coordinating the time and place to meet up, we recommend you this way. Just join meetups and say hello, it's done.

For local people

1. Register your profile and wait for requests from foreign traveler

Just register your profile, foreign people will find you and ask you to have local dining together. Then, just talk with them about their interest and and the place and time to meet up.

2. Join meetups created by other users

You can find meetups which you can join with "Find meetups" feature of Relicious If you don't feel like finding people and coordinating the time and place to meet up, we recommend you this way. Just join meetups and say hello, it's done.
